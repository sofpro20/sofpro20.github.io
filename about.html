About me

I was born and raised in Nikolaev - a small town in Ukraine. From childhood, my parents instilled in me a love of travel and at the age of seven years, they began to show me various cities and sights in different parts of Ukraine. I visited more than 15 cities and found the most magnificent and historical places in each. But as I grew older, I began to feel the desire to travel on my own - and in my 20s, I went on my trip to France, Paris. For me, everything was new, as I shared emotions with myself, chose what I want to visit and enjoyed the views without haste. Before my 20th birthday, I visited more than 20 countries with my family such as the Emirates, Bulgaria, Spain, Italy, Hungary, Poland and others. Each captivated me with its diversity and culture, which was unlike any of the previous ones. The field of travel inspired me so much that now I work in a travel company and help people fulfill their desires and visit places where they feel incredible emotions.
